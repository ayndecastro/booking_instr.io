OPENLIVE

So you heard that Band Barracks now offers booking with openLIVE is a hi-res music recording and distribution platform and are wondering "how do I get my hands on that?"


Here's 4 simple steps to get you going!

  • STEP #1

    visit Band barracks booking page and book an available room.


  • STEP #2

    fill out all you information and make sure to check the box that says "Book the room with openLIVE session."

    fill out your payment information then click "Confirm Booking"


  • STEP #3

    Confirm your Band Barracks (powered by Skedda) booking account email and create a password.


  • STEP #4

    You will recieve 2 emails from openLIVE. One is a recording session confirmation


    The other one will be your account confirmation. Confirm your email by clicking the link in the email and use your email address and temporary password that we sent you with a subject of openLIVE Credentials

    Double check your openLIVE recording session and make sure that is in sync with you Band Barracks booking.

    Everything looks fine? make sure to change your temporary password right away. This is very important and make sure you do not skip this step.

  • STEP #5

    Now sitdown. Relax. and Wait. openLIVE will do everything. it will automatically start/stop recording based on your booking's start and finish time.
