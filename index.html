So you heard that Band Barracks now offers booking with openLIVE and are wondering "how do I get my hands on that?"
Here's 4 simple steps to get you going!


FIRST STEP: Choose a date, room, and time

visit Band barracks booking page by clicking the button bellow. You will see the current date on the top of the page, the name of the rooms on each column and the available time in each row divided per hour. To book a room, simply click on the current date on the top of the page and select a date from the calendar pop-up. Then select a time slot under the column of the room you want to book then hit "Book". Once that's done enter your email address in the pop-up and hit next.


Link to Booking Page

FILL OUT YOUR INFORMATION

In the next pop-up you will be prompted to fill in your information. You'll notice that there is a check box that says "Book the room with openLIVE session." Make sure you check that box to automatically book you openLIVE session. Then fill in your payment information, review everything and hit confirm booking after aggreeing to the terms and policy.


That's it! You've just booked your first openLIVE session. The next step is making sure that you did everything right!

CONFIRM YOUR BOOKING

You will recieve five emails. Three from Band Barracks and two from openLIVE. If you don't see them in your inbox make sure to check your spam folder. First open the email from Band Barracks with the subject "Confirm your new account" link will redirect you to a login set-up page. You may choose to login with a password you create or any of the other third-party applications listed on the right side.

Next, go back to your email and open the email from openLIVE with the subject "Confirm your email" and click the link which will redirect you to the openLIVE login. login with the email address you used too book your session and use the temporary password that Band Barracks sent you under the subject "openLIVE credentials." Once you're in, you may check your openLIVE scheduled session under your artist dashboard. Make sure all the informations' right and that's it!

EXTRA: BUT ALSO IMPORTANT

The two remaining emails are your Band Barracks booking confirmation and openLIVE session confirmation emails. Make sure you review the information in each email and that the time and date matches in both.


This extra step is for your security. Since we sent you a temporary password in plain text, your password is exposed. Under your openLIVE account settings go to password reset and change your password to anything you like. That's it, you're truly done! all that's left to do is sit back and wait for your booking date.
